TWAN KANON

The product of Twan Kanon's eccentric mind, Twan Kanon Studios is a place where photographers, artist and creatives can come to work in a comfortable & quality environment. With his passion for photography and knowledge of films Twan Kanon acts as a gate keeper for some of atlanta's rising talent. Located on the westside of Atlanta its not hard to see why his style embodies that gritty and urban feel.The studio is fully equipped with a lighting package, a natural light studio, backdrop of choice, changing area, rest room, and wifi.
